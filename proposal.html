Cherry: Health Buddy Tracking Solution

Problem Description

Being able to manage one's health can become a daunting task nowadays. We usually rely on hospitals and clinics to manage our health records and those sometimes are not shared across these different institutions. We believe that patients should have control over their health record, however, there is a gap that exists between health institutions and its patients. Yes, there are solutions that already exist that are provided by health institutions, however we have found that a common problem across these is that they are under utilized because they are complex, not user-friendly, and often unengaging.

It is crucial to manage one's health, but it is difficult with the portals and apps that exist to maintain that self engagement and accountability in our own health. These lack personalization, or overall lack a good interface. Our proposed web app will aim to bridge this gap by offering a personalized experience through an initial quiz to understand user's preferences and even treatment needs, along with features that will allow for the categorization of their habits in a user-friendly manner. Users will also be able to journal and their experiences. All of this seeks to make a significant difference in the lives of those striving to manage their health in a more effective way.

Existing Attempts

Currently, habit trackers online exist. For example, DailyHabits tracks users' habits and displays them in a calendar. DailyHabits displays the number of habits users have achieved and their habit goal. Another wellness checking option is MyChart. MyChart includes the ability to contact your doctor, view test results, and manage appointments. While myChart encompasses aspects of health and wellness, it doesn't necessarily support long term health goals, specifically building healthy habits that support certain diagnoses.

App Proposal

On our app users will be interacting with their personally uploaded data about their habits. Our app will allow for the addition of that information as well as have a dashboard to access a summary of their progress on keeping up with these habits. Our app would synthesize the daily information into forms generally useful to our users; for instance into weekly/monthly charts or statistics including average time spent on any certain habit.

Finally we identified a problem, the lack of an approachable habit tracker apps especially in the context of medical treatment. Our app aims to address said issue by providing a very flexible but still attractive app for housing all of your habit information. Our app will be a low stakes affair allowing people to add however much or however little information they want to everyday without penalty. Our app will support a person's existing intrinsic motivation to better themselves, and create a space for self-organization which is especially desired with an audience of patients.

App Description

Our ideal audience of this application is patients who are looking to implement a daily routine to improve/maintain their health diagnosis. Based on their assigned treatment plan, this web app allows users to create a standardized daily schedule. Users have access to certain features that help them align themselves to the goals they have set.

  • Introductory Quiz: Users start off with a quiz to collect information on their preferences, frequency of treatment steps, and what they'd like out of their experience. This generates a sample daily checklist.
  • Organize Daily Checklist: Users will have the ability to organize their daily checklist, through features like grouping by category/week and adding more habits to certain days.
  • Progress Inputting: Allows users to input their progress throughout the day for each task.
  • Daily/Weekly Reflection: Fill out an optional daily/weekly journaling section that allows them to reflect on their progress and note down any important thoughts.

Overall, these features allow users to input, edit and keep track of their habits to keep them on top of their treatment plans.
